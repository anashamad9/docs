Workspace settings let you control how your workspace is identified and how the Atmet interface looks and behaves for everyone in it. To open Workspace settings, click **Settings** in the bottom-left corner of the sidebar, then select **Workspace** from the left panel. If you belong to more than one workspace, use the workspace switcher at the top of the sidebar to select the one you want to configure before opening Settings.

## Workspace profile

The workspace profile controls the name, logo, and region that appear throughout the platform — in the workspace switcher, in invitation emails, and in admin views.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Workspace settings">
    Click **Settings** in the bottom-left of the sidebar. Select **Workspace** from the left panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update the workspace name">
    Edit the **Workspace name** field. The name you enter appears in the sidebar workspace switcher and in any invitation emails sent to new members.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload a workspace avatar">
    Hover over the workspace logo in the top-left of the panel. Click the edit icon, then choose **Upload image** to select a logo from your device, or **Delete image** to remove it. If no image is set, the platform shows a two-letter abbreviation of the workspace name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the workspace country">
    The **Country** field reflects the country associated with the workspace owner's profile. The supported countries are: Jordan, United Arab Emirates, Saudi Arabia, United States, United Kingdom, Germany, France, Canada, Australia, and Singapore. This field is derived from the owner's account and is read-only in Workspace settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Click **Save** at the bottom-right of the panel. The button enables only when there are pending changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Primary email

The **Primary email** field shows the email address of the workspace owner. It is read-only. If you need to update it, contact Atmet support.

## Appearance

The Appearance section controls the visual theme of the Atmet interface. These settings are personal — they apply to your view only and do not affect what other members see.

<Tip>
  Workspace-level settings such as name and country apply to all members. Appearance settings — theme mode, accent color, and font scale — are stored per user in your browser and do not affect your teammates' experience.
</Tip>

### Theme mode

Choose one of three theme modes:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Light" icon="sun">
    A white background with dark text. Well suited to bright environments and extended daytime use.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Dark" icon="moon">
    A dark background with light text. Reduces eye strain in dim environments.
  </Card>
  <Card title="System" icon="display">
    Automatically matches your operating system's current appearance preference. Switches between Light and Dark when your system does.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Click the theme preview card that matches your preference. The change applies immediately without requiring you to save.

### Accent color

Select a primary accent color to apply to buttons, focused field borders, and interactive elements across the interface. The available colors are:

| Color | Hex |
|---|---|
| Graphite | `#111111` |
| Cobalt | `#1e90ff` |
| Emerald | `#059669` |
| Violet | `#7c3aed` |
| Rose | `#e11d48` |
| Amber | `#d97706` |
| Cyan | `#0891b2` |
| Teal | `#0f766e` |
| Slate | `#334155` |

Click a color swatch to apply it. The change takes effect immediately and persists across sessions in your browser.

### Font scale

Adjust the global text size across the entire Atmet interface:

- **Smaller** — reduces the base font size to 15 px
- **Default** — uses the standard base font size
- **Bigger** — increases the base font size to 17 px

Select your preferred scale and click **Save** to apply it.

## Time and language

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set your timezone">
    Open the **Time zone** dropdown and select the timezone that matches your location. Atmet uses this setting to display timestamps and schedule-related information in the right local time. The available options include Asia/Amman, Asia/Dubai, Asia/Riyadh, Europe/London, UTC, America/New_York, America/Los_Angeles, and Asia/Tokyo. If your system timezone is detected automatically, it appears at the top of the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your language">
    Open the **Language** dropdown and select your preferred interface language. Supported languages are English, Arabic, French, Spanish, German, and Japanese.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** at the bottom-right of the panel to apply your time and language changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Notification preferences

Control which events trigger notifications and how frequently you receive summary digests.

### Notification categories

You can independently toggle email and in-app notifications for each of the following categories:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Security alerts" icon="key">
    Notifications for sign-ins, password changes, and other security-related events on your account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Product updates" icon="briefcase">
    Announcements about new features, improvements, and changes to the Atmet platform.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tips and recommendations" icon="bell">
    Helpful suggestions and usage tips from the Atmet team to help you get more from the platform.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

For each category, check the **Email** checkbox to receive email notifications, and the **In-app** checkbox to receive in-app notifications. Both can be enabled, both can be disabled, or each can be set independently.

### Digest frequency

Choose how often Atmet batches non-urgent notifications into a digest:

- **Real-time** — sends each notification as it occurs
- **Daily digest** — bundles notifications into one email per day
- **Weekly digest** — bundles notifications into one email per week
- **Off** — disables digest emails entirely

### Quiet hours

Enable **Quiet hours** to pause notifications during a specific window of time each day. Toggle the **Quiet hours** checkbox, then set the start time and end time using the time pickers. Notifications generated during quiet hours are held and delivered once the window ends.

Click **Save** to apply your notification preferences.

## Members and workspace deletion

The Workspace settings panel also provides quick links to adjacent management areas.

- Click **Go to members** to open the Members settings panel, where you can invite or remove teammates and manage roles.
- The **Delete workspace** button in the Danger Zone permanently removes the workspace and all associated data. Only the workspace Owner can perform this action.

<Warning>
  Deleting a workspace permanently removes all chats, workflows, files, integrations, and member access associated with it. This action cannot be undone.
</Warning>
