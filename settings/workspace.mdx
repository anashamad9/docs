Workspace settings let you control how your workspace is identified across the Atmet platform — its name, logo, description, and the region it is associated with. To open Workspace settings, click **Settings** in the bottom-left corner of the sidebar, then select **Workspace** from the left panel. If you belong to more than one workspace, use the workspace switcher at the top of the sidebar to select the one you want to configure before opening Settings.

## Workspace profile

The workspace profile controls the name, logo, description, and region that appear throughout the platform — in the workspace switcher, in invitation emails, and in admin views.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Workspace settings">
    Click **Settings** in the bottom-left of the sidebar. Select **Workspace** from the left panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update the workspace name">
    Edit the **Workspace name** field. The name you enter appears in the sidebar workspace switcher and in any invitation emails sent to new members.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload a workspace avatar">
    Hover over the workspace logo in the top-left of the panel. Click the edit icon, then choose **Upload image** to select a logo from your device, or **Delete image** to remove it. If no image is set, the platform shows a two-letter abbreviation of the workspace name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a description">
    Enter a short description in the **Description** field to summarize the purpose or team behind the workspace. This is optional and visible to workspace members.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the workspace country">
    The **Country** field shows the country associated with the workspace owner's profile. The supported countries are: Jordan, United Arab Emirates, Saudi Arabia, United States, United Kingdom, Germany, France, Canada, Australia, and Singapore. This field is derived from the owner's account and is read-only in Workspace settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Click **Save** at the bottom-right of the panel. The button enables only when there are pending changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Primary email

The **Primary email** field shows the email address of the workspace owner. It is read-only. If you need to update it, contact Atmet support.

## Members and workspace deletion

The Workspace settings panel also provides quick links to adjacent management areas.

- Click **Go to members** to open the Members settings panel, where you can invite or remove teammates and manage roles.
- The **Delete workspace** button in the Danger Zone permanently removes the workspace and all associated data. Only the workspace Owner can perform this action.

<Warning>
  Deleting a workspace permanently removes all chats, workflows, files, integrations, and member access associated with it. This action cannot be undone.
</Warning>

## Appearance and display preferences

Theme mode, accent color, font scale, timezone, and language are personal display preferences — they apply to your view only and do not affect what other members see. You can find these options under **General** in the Settings panel.

<Tip>
  To change your interface theme, accent color, or font size, open Settings and select **General** from the left panel. To configure notification preferences, select **Notifications**.
</Tip>
