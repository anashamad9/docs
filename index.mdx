## How Atmet works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your workspace">
    Sign up and create a workspace for your team. Invite members and assign roles to control who can build and run automations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your apps">
    Link Gmail, Slack, Notion, HubSpot, GitHub, and more from the Apps page. Each integration takes under two minutes to set up.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Chat to build workflows">
    Open AI Core and describe what you want to automate in plain language. The AI agent turns your description into a structured workflow with trigger and action steps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add skills and run">
    Attach reusable skills to enhance the AI's capabilities, then publish and run your workflow manually or on a schedule.
  </Step>
</Steps>
